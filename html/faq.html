Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We've got answers! If you can't find the answer you're looking for, please contact us.

How long will it take to repair my device?

Most repairs take our technicians an hour to complete. However, some repairs may take longer. We will always keep you updated on the progress of your repair.

Do you have a repair warranty on your services?

Yes, We offer a 90-day warranty on all repairs. If you have any issues with your repair, please contact us and we will be happy to help.

What devices do you offer repair services for?

We offer repair services for all major brands of smartphones, tablets, and laptops. We also offer repair services for watches, gaming consoles, and more.

Do you sell screen protectors and cases?

Yes, we do! We offer a wide variety of screen protectors and cases for all major brands of smartphones, tablets, and laptops.

Do you offer mail-in repair services?

Yes, we do! We offer mail-in repair services for all major brands of smartphones, tablets, and laptops. Please contact us for more information.

Do you offer a discount for first service?

Yes, we do! We offer a 10% discount on your first repair service. Please mention this offer when you call or visit us.

Do you sell Smartphones, Watches, Laptops, and Tablets?

Yes, we sell all major brands of smartphones, tablets, and laptops. We also sell smartwatches and gaming consoles.

How to find you stores near me?

You can find our stores near you by visiting our store locator page. You can also find our stores by searching for us on Google Maps.

Do you offer a discount for students?

Yes, we do! We offer a 30% discount on all repair services for students.For that you need to submit your ID.Please mention this offer when you call or visit us.
